Courses Offered at PVPSIT

Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses designed to provide in-depth knowledge in the fields of engineering, technology, and management.

B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

This program equips students with knowledge in computer science fundamentals, programming, and software development.

B.Tech in Information Technology (IT)

Focuses on programming, networking, and databases for IT industries.

B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Designed to teach communication systems, embedded systems, and signal processing.

B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML)

Prepares students for careers in AI, machine learning, and data science.

B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering

Covers mechanics, thermodynamics, and robotics for system development.
